Privacy Policy

Zaychess does not collect analytics or tracking data.

Summary

  • No account required.
  • No analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.
  • Game data stays on your device.

Data you provide

If you contact support, you may provide your name, email, and message through the support form. This information is used only to respond to your request and is not sold or shared.

Network use

Multiplayer uses direct TCP connections between players. There are no relay servers and no gameplay telemetry is collected.

Local storage

Zaychess stores saved games (.chesslog) and preferences locally on your machine.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.

Contact

Questions? Use the support form at jeremyzay.com/zaychess/support.
